Research

I study strongly correlated phases of electrons and bosons in flat Chern bands and moiré materials. My work explores how interactions, topology, and quantum geometry conspire to produce fractionalized liquids, charge order, and unconventional superconductivity. A current focus is building and benchmarking neural-network variational wavefunctions (NN‑VMC) that respect magnetic translation symmetries on the torus.
